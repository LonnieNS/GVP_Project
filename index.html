MEET 'GEEK'

aka. B. Ray Pennington

Geek is a Florida based jack of all trades, with strengths spanning both visual art, music and entertainment. Living the struggling-artist life to the fullest, he can be found roaming the Pensacola art scene. Wether it’s local markets or gallery nights, he’ll be there, ready to present and sell his work. While his daily-life doesn’t include the Gator-wrestling chaos that we’ve come to expect from Florida men, his art certainly does. Embracing absurdity and his ever-present adolescent sense of humor, Geek is inspired by his passion for retro gaming and … well … anything that makes him giggle.
